Designed in London, made by Italian hands

Collection Noir Atelier was founded in London in 2020 by Samantha Santini, an interior designer with over ten years specifying furniture for private clients before founding the brand.

Every commission begins as a drawing in the London atelier. From there, it's realised by hands trained over generations — Italian craftsmanship closes that gap. It always has.

The collection extends beyond tables into case goods — bedside tables and storage pieces that bring stone, timber and bronze together. Every piece is made to order.
