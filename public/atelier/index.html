Collection Atelier Trade Enquire

Designed in London. Made by Italian hands.

Collection Noir makes bespoke furniture in marble and stone for those who see material as a form of permanence. Each piece is drawn in London and carved by hand in Italy, one commission at a time.

No two pieces leave the atelier the same. That is craft, not inconsistency.

Every commission begins as a drawing in the London atelier. From there, it is realised by hands trained over generations. The collection extends beyond tables into case goods: bedside tables and storage pieces that bring stone, timber and bronze together. Every piece is made to order.

“I spent years specifying furniture for clients and consistently finding that what existed in the market either compromised on material or on design. Collection Noir exists because that gap should not.”

Samantha Santini

Founder & Designer, Collection Noir Atelier, London
